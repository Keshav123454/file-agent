The lion is one of the most powerful animals in the wild.
It is often called the “King of the Jungle.”
Lions mainly live in grasslands and savannas of Africa.
A male lion is easily recognized by its thick mane.
Lions are carnivores and hunt animals like deer and zebras.
They live in groups called prides.
Female lions usually do most of the hunting.
Lions are known for their loud and powerful roar.
A lion’s roar can be heard from several kilometers away.
They are strong, fast, and skilled hunters.
Lions spend much of their day resting and sleeping.
They play an important role in maintaining ecological balance.
Sadly, lions are facing threats due to habitat loss and hunting.
Conservation efforts are being made to protect them.
The lion symbolizes courage, strength, and leadership.
